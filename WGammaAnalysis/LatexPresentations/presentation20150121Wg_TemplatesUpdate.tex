\begin{frame}{Regarding Wg MC closure test:}\\*
  \scriptsize I found the problem why fits on MC closure test on Wg+Wjets always failed: I made the parameters nTrue and nFake to vary in range from 0 to max=treeData-$>$GetEntries(cut). While that's proper definition for fits on real data, for MC mixture where Wjets dominates and is weighted by ~10, such max is too small.\\*
  \scriptsize Fits still fail sometimes (the same as they do for data or Zg) with the following minuit complain:\\*
  \scriptsize - - - - - - - - - \\*
  \scriptsize [#0] WARNING:Minization -- RooFitGlue: Minimized function has error status.
Returning maximum FCN so far (-1000000000000000019884624838656) to force MIGRAD to back out of this region. Error log follows
Parameter values: nFake=3494, nTrue=878\\*
RooAddPdf::fitModel[ nTrue * truePdf + nFake * fakePdf ]\\*
     getLogVal() top-level p.d.f evaluates to zero @ !refCoefNorm=(), !pdfs=(truePdf = 0/4364.85,fakePdf = 0/2836.69), !coefficients=(nTrue = 877.531,nFake = 3493.76)\\*
  \scriptsize - - - - - - - - - \\*
  \scriptsize This can be avoided with changing number of fit bins ar fit range.\\*
\end{frame}
